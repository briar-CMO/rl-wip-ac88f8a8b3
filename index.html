Format: 80% of spend is UGC, 20% static — yet the three best ROAS ads are all statics (Hair Mask 7.1x, Hair Ugly 7.2x, Spotlight 4.7x). Audience: 65% prospecting, 18% existing, with thinning-hair audiences only ~7% combined.

3. The gaps to fill next

  • Under-weighting statics. 80% of spend is UGC, but Hair Mask (7.1x), Hair Ugly (7.2x) and Spotlight (4.7x) statics are the top ROAS. Shift more budget to statics.
  • No objection / safety angle in the spend — yet ‘prostaglandin-free’ converts in search. (Concept 2’s Sensitive route + a dedicated static.)
  • No competitor-comparison angle — grandelash, rapidlash and lilash convert in search. Conquest creative is missing.
  • No founder / credibility angle in the current mix — ‘physician-developed, since 2006’ (Concept 4) builds trust the offer-ads can’t.
  • Cut the dead weight: Briar Lash & Brow UGC has fallen to 0.70x / $185 CPA — covered by your budget cut.

4. Who we’re speaking to

The Age-Aware 45–60
Lashes & brows thinned with age / menopause. Top-spending ROAS demo.
Driver: Look like myself again, not ‘done’.
What lands: Before/after result, awards, ‘physician-developed since 2006’.
The Young Maximalist 18–34
Wants longer, fuller, ‘fake-looking’ lashes without extensions. Highest ROAS by age.
Driver: Beauty & identity; TikTok-native.
What lands: UGC demo, ‘people think my lashes are fake’, mascara, trial kit.
The Hair-Thinning Woman 35+
Shedding / thinning hair (postpartum, stress, age). High ad engagement.
Driver: Anxiety about visible thinning.
What lands: ‘Hair Ugly’ pain+result, hairdresser approval, the new mask.
The Ingredient-Cautious 30–55
