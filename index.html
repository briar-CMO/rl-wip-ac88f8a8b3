Open decisions for the week

  • Budget held back into target. Prospecting campaign off, Google stepped to $40/day and Meta to $200/day. June still lands ~10% over target at a ~5x return.
  • Scale the statics. Hair Ugly (7.4x), Before & After (7.3x), Spotlight (5.6x) and the new Hair Mask static (5.4x) are our best ROAS at tiny spend. Brief more.
  • Refresh the fatigued winners, do not kill them. Viral UGC Mascara and Brows UGC have huge lifetime spend; recent dips are fatigue, not a dead angle.
  • Ingredient-safety angle (prostaglandin-free) is still an open creative gap worth a test.
  • Read-only Shopify access (Annemarie → BCUN) so NZ revenue reports automatically instead of being entered by hand.

What is winning

Two UGC workhorses carry the spend (Viral Mascara at $1,805 and Free Gifts at $1,176), both still profitable but fatiguing on a lifetime view. The real signal is the statics: Hair Ugly (7.4x), Before & After (7.3x), Spotlight (5.6x) and the new Hair Mask static (5.4x) are the most efficient creative in the account on a fraction of the budget. The angle mix is heavy on Social Proof and Promotional Offers; Guaranteed-Results statics are the under-funded winner.
